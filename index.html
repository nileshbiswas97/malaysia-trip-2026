💳 Money dos & don'ts

✓ Do
  • Use a zero-forex-markup card for most payments (GrabPay/cards everywhere)
  • Withdraw RM from Maybank/CIMB ATMs (lower fees) in one-two big pulls
  • Shop duty-free in Langkawi — chocolates, perfume, alcohol at ~60–70% of mainland prices
✗ Don't
  • Don't exchange at airport counters (worst rates) — small amount only
  • Don't accept "pay in INR?" on card machines (dynamic conversion = 5–8% hidden fee) — always pay in MYR
  • Don't carry >RM10k cash through customs (declaration needed)
